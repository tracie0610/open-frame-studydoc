公告: JBoss.org releases new wiki and community collaboration features
  • 显示详情
  • 隐藏详情

The JBoss.org team are proud to announce the release of our new wiki together with additional community collaboration features!

 

In our continued efforts to grow the open community around JBoss.org projects we have migrated all of our existing wiki data and user profiles into a new Clearspace server. The move allows us to take advantage of modern collaboration features such as comments, tagging, RSS feeds, email notifications, and content ratings in addition to improving the basic functionality of the wiki.

 

Simply login as normal using your jboss.org username/password or register for an account using the links above.

 

Enjoy!
